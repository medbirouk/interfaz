HISTORY

"Raja Club Athletic, known internationally as Raja Casablanca , is a professional football club located in Casablanca , Morocco. It plays in the Moroccan First Division , the country's highest category. It was founded on March 20, 1949 by various social organizations linked to the Moroccan resistance. They are one of the most important teams in Moroccan football and have earned a reputation for playing with an attractive and colorful style."
